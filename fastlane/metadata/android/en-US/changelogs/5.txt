v1.2.0 — reliable background timers

• Foreground service keeps the app's timer logic alive while the screen
  is locked or the app is in recents. Pomodoro, countdown and stopwatch
  notifications and sounds now fire correctly when the phone is locked
  or backgrounded (no more reliance on battery-restricted scheduled
  alarms). A small low-priority "Tympeak — timer active" notification
  appears while any timer is running and disappears on reset / finish.
• Pomodoro completion notification now fires reliably (was racing with
  the scheduled-alarm cancel and silently dismissing itself).
