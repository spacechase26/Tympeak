v1.3.0 — survives app being swiped from recents

• Replaced the bare-bones foreground service with a dedicated background
  Dart isolate (via flutter_foreground_task). The new isolate keeps
  running independently of the main UI isolate, so timers continue to
  fire notifications even when the app is force-closed from the recents
  list on Xiaomi / MIUI / OneUI and other aggressive task killers.
• Live chronometer, segment transitions and final completion alerts are
  now all owned by the background isolate, eliminating the previous
  "two isolates fighting over the same notification" races.
