A no-nonsense productivity app — built in a day, refined into a final release.

<b>Tasks</b>
• Priorities, due dates, recurring days, custom reminder times
• Swipe to delete with confirmation
• Notifications fire even when the app is closed

<b>Habits</b>
• Yes/no, countable, and timer-based habit types
• Streaks, weekly heatmap, confetti on a perfect day
• Import / export as CSV

<b>Pomodoro Timer</b>
• Editable focus, break, and loop count
• Quick-start countdown presets (1m, 5m, 10m, 15m, 25m, 45m, 60m, custom)
• Stopwatch with laps
• Timers keep running even when the app is killed — scheduled notifications fire at the exact moment

<b>Notes</b>
• Daily Journal with mood tracking and writing-streak badge
• Quick Notes with autosave, search, pin, archive, and markdown editor
• Export / import the whole notes box as JSON

<b>Privacy</b>
• 100% offline — no accounts, no cloud, no analytics, no tracking, no internet permission
• All data stored locally with Hive
• Open source

Glassmorphism dark UI, floating nav bar, edge-to-edge. Designed so every action takes minimal taps.
